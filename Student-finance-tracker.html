Student Finance Tracker

About This Project

The Student Finance Tracker helps you manage your personal budget by recording expenses, viewing spending trends, and customizing categories for your daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly expenses.

Sections Overview:

  • Dashboard / Stats: See summaries and charts of your spending.
  • Records: View all your transactions and filter by date range.
  • Add/Edit Categories: Create and manage your own expense categories.
  • Settings: Choose your preferred currency and display mode (light/dark).
